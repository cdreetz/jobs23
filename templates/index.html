Skills Comparison Tool

This tool allows you to compare various skill sets and understand the nuances between different job roles. Navigate through the tool below to get started.

The results are the product of a collection of hundred of job postings and their descriptions stored in our database. When you select a specific role the information on skill relevancy will be specific to the jobs with that title.
